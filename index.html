WHO WE ARE

The heat ritual
is ten thousand
years old.

Danu was the mother goddess of the ancient Celts — the giver of warmth, the holder of waters. We named our saunas after her because we believe in the same thing she stood for: the slow, necessary reclamation of your own body.

The sauna is not a product. It is a practice. We started Danu because we thought Irish and British gardens deserved something better than flat-pack timber boxes from a catalogue. Every sauna we make is handcrafted from sustainably sourced wood and installed by our own team — not subcontractors, not couriers, our people. From the first phone call to the moment you step inside for the first time, we are with you.

Reset your nervous system

Twenty minutes in a sauna reduces cortisol levels by up to 32%. Heat exposure triggers the release of endorphins — your body's natural mechanism for dissolving stress at a physiological level.

3×

Deeper, longer sleep

Regular sauna use has been shown to improve deep sleep duration by up to three times. The post-heat cool-down drops your core temperature sharply — the same signal your body uses to initiate deep sleep.

10,000

Years of practice

The sauna tradition dates back over ten millennia. It is one of the oldest wellness practices in human history — and modern science is only now beginning to quantify what our ancestors understood instinctively.
